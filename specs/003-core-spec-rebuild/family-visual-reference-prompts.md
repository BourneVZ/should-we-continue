# Family Visual Reference Prompts

更新时间：2026-06-28

本文档用于第二轮家族视觉分化试验。
目标不是继续微调旧海报，而是直接打破四个家族当前“同模板 + 同色系 + 同清单墙”的问题。

本轮只先做 4 张参考图：
- `CTRL`：控盘家族
- `BASE`：地基家族
- `HOLD`：献祭家族
- `GLOW`：投射家族

## Unified Style Base

以下内容四张图共用：

```text
Single full-body female character poster for the MMTI project.
Stylized illustration with crisp faceted geometry and intentional graphic design.
Readable title area at the top with archetype code and Chinese title.
Not photorealistic, not anime, not maternal-brand commercial softness.
Humorous and sharp, but not cruel or grotesque.
Poster-grade composition with strong silhouette and a clean focal point.
```

本轮特别新增的统一约束：

```text
Do not default to pinned checklists, sticky-note walls, or repeated paper cards.
Do not default to olive-beige palette.
Each family must be visually separable at first glance by palette, composition, prop language, and body energy.
```

## CTRL

### Family Style Base

```text
Control family visual language:
sharp geometry, sparse composition, command-center clarity, tactical posture.
Palette: graphite, warm ivory, muted crimson, brass accents.
Background: abstract dashboard rings, directional arrows, thin control lines, structured panels.
Prop language: baton, command board, tactical overlays, signal markers.
Avoid domestic clutter and avoid soft emotional decoration.
```

### Specific Archetype Layer

```text
Archetype: CTRL, Chinese title 控盘总监.
She looks like the person who enters chaos and immediately starts structuring the room.
The first memory point is not “many lists,” but “she herself feels like the command system.”
Her body language should suggest interception, rerouting, and executive prioritization.
```

### Face Lock Layer

```text
Tall lean build, long limbs, angular shoulders.
Diamond-shaped face, sharp jawline, light warm skin.
Narrow lifted eyes, straight strong brows, defined nose bridge, controlled half-smile.
Dark hair in a sleek low ponytail with one precise side part.
```

### Negative Constraints

```text
No sticky-note wall.
No overloaded tote bags.
No cozy dream props.
No construction-worker heaviness.
Not the same face as other archetypes.
Do not reuse the same woman template.
```

## BASE

### Family Style Base

```text
Foundation family visual language:
low center of gravity, blocky weight, grounded realism, structural support.
Palette: steel blue, concrete gray, ochre, brick rust.
Background: beams, load blocks, braces, cross-sections, weight markers.
Prop language: measuring tools, support modules, bearing structures, material samples.
Avoid glamour, avoid dream haze, avoid airy floating composition.
```

### Specific Archetype Layer

```text
Archetype: BASE, Chinese title 地基会计师.
She looks like someone who checks whether life can bear the load before allowing optimism in.
The first memory point is not paperwork, but “she is standing inside a structure and testing its bearing capacity.”
Her visual energy should feel like stability, realism, and non-negotiable load math.
```

### Face Lock Layer

```text
Medium height, sturdy build, heavy stance.
Broad rectangular face, medium deep tan skin.
Focused hooded eyes, heavy flat brows, wide blunt nose, firm mouth.
Hair in a practical braided ponytail with blunt short bangs.
```

### Negative Constraints

```text
No executive command-board vibe.
No floating dreamy frames.
No martyrdom clutter.
No repeated list-paper motif.
Not the same face as other archetypes.
Do not reuse the same woman template.
```

## HOLD

### Family Style Base

```text
Sacrifice family visual language:
compressed shoulders, asymmetrical burden, too many carried necessities, visible emotional labor.
Palette: terracotta, faded wine, dusty brown, oatmeal, muted plum accents.
Background: straps, bags, stacked soft supplies, hanging burdens, half-open emergency gear.
Prop language: backup supplies, support objects, rolled blankets, practical care items, things for other people.
Avoid polished control-room minimalism and avoid dreamy symbolic space.
```

### Specific Archetype Layer

```text
Archetype: HOLD, Chinese title 扛事预备役.
She looks like the person who started preparing for everyone else's collapse before anyone even asked.
The first memory point is not a checklist, but “her body is already carrying the contingency plan.”
Her silhouette should feel burdened but reliable, warm but visibly overused.
```

### Face Lock Layer

```text
Short sturdy build, slightly sloped shoulders, solid legs.
Soft square face, sun-warmed skin, tired almond eyes.
Thick soft brows, broad short nose, dry slightly downturned lips.
Dense dark hair in a heavy side braid with a cloth headband.
```

### Negative Constraints

```text
No crisp executive posture.
No architectural worksite language.
No floating fantasy frames.
No repeated pinned paper wall.
Not the same face as other archetypes.
Do not reuse the same woman template.
```

## GLOW

### Family Style Base

```text
Projection family visual language:
lightness, translucency, luminous air, symbolic future imagery, emotional atmosphere before logistics.
Palette: pearl cream, soft gold, celadon, pale sky blue, faint lilac accents.
Background: floating frames, glass charms, light orbs, reflective windows, imagined scenes.
Prop language: glow objects, keepsakes, vision fragments, atmosphere artifacts.
Avoid worksite realism, command systems, and burden-clutter silhouettes.
```

### Specific Archetype Layer

```text
Archetype: GLOW, Chinese title 母职滤镜师.
She looks like someone who falls in love with the imagined emotional glow of the future before the future is built.
The first memory point is not “she has plans,” but “she is illuminated by the versions of life she is already seeing.”
Her visual energy should feel airy, hopeful, aesthetic, and slightly self-hypnotized.
```

### Face Lock Layer

```text
Tall slim build, elongated neck, soft oval face.
Very fair warm skin with freckles.
Large reflective eyes, fine curved brows, delicate nose, small bright mouth.
Long loose braid mixed with ribbon and small charm details.
```

### Negative Constraints

```text
No clipboard or command-center props.
No construction beams or weight charts.
No overloaded martyr packs.
No repeated checklist wall.
Not the same face as other archetypes.
Do not reuse the same woman template.
```
